About

I am a television producer and creative with over fifteen years of experience working across late-night television, comedy, production, and creative development.

My career began with internships at 30 Rock, Saturday Night Live, and The Onion News Network before moving into production and post-production roles on The Michael J. Fox Show and Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt.

Most recently, I spent a decade at The Late Show with Stephen Colbert, where I worked my way from Production Assistant to Segment Producer, producing comedy segments, video content, musical parodies, and pre-taped pieces in a fast-paced daily production environment.

Outside of television, I enjoy photography, storytelling, archives, technology, and creative projects of all kinds.

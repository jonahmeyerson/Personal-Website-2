Welcome!

My name is Jonah Meyerson. I'm a television producer, storyteller, and creative problem solver.

For more than fifteen years, I've worked in television production, helping bring ideas from page to screen. Most recently, I worked as a Segment Producer for The Late Show with Stephen Colbert, where I produced comedy segments, animations, video packages, musical parodies, and day-of creative content for a nightly audience of millions.

Over the course of my career, I've contributed to more than 2,000 episodes of television across production, post-production, and creative development roles. I enjoy projects that sit at the intersection of comedy, storytelling, creative development, and production. I love the collaborative process and bringing creative work to life.

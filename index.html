Welcome!

My name is Jonah Meyerson. I'm a television producer and creative storyteller with more than 15 years of experience.

I've worked on over 2,000 episodes of television across production, post-production, and creative development roles. Most recently, I spent a decade at The Late Show with Stephen Colbert, producing daily live studio comedy, original videos, animations, musical parodies, and creative content for a nightly audience of millions.

I specialize in projects that sit at the intersection of storytelling, creative development, comedy, and production. I love collaborating with talented, creative people, bringing ideas to life, and making fun things.

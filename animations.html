Animations

Animation has always been one of my favorite storytelling tools. Whether it's a Christmas-themed satire of the invasion of Ukraine or a chorus line of dancing syringes singing hip-hop, animation offers endless opportunities for creativity, humor, and visual storytelling.

When producing animations, I helped develop concepts and visual ideas, recorded voice-overs and managed recording sessions, edited and mixed audio, and worked closely with writers and animators to shepherd each piece from launch to final delivery.
